K-Pop Idol Face Identifier

An app that identifies Korean Pop idols from the image that the user posts/uploads.

It is built by Angular.JS, Node.JS, Express, Microsoft Cognitive Services API, and HTML/CSS.

Thirty images per each celebrity were used to train the model. Currently, there are 78 celebrities available to identify and the app has 85% guaranteed accuracy.
